BambooCraft Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025 • web.bamboocraftmc.net

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy explains how BambooCraft ("we," "our," or "us") handles player information on our Minecraft server and related services.

  • Server Name: BambooCraft
  • Website: web.bamboocraftmc.net
  • Contact Email: sbamboomcnetwork@gmail.com
  • Location: United States

By joining our server or using our related services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you play on BambooCraft, the following types of data may be collected automatically:

  • Minecraft username and UUID — Used to identify your player account.
  • IP address — Used for network security and banning purposes. IPs are stored in a way that cannot be linked directly to usernames.
  • Chat and command logs — Stored for moderation and safety purposes.
  • Gameplay data — Actions performed in-game (for example, block placements or interactions) may be logged automatically by server plugins.
  • Discord IDs — May be temporarily recorded if you link your Minecraft and Discord accounts or interact via our Discord server. Discord stores many interaction logs on its own platform.
  • Payment information — Handled entirely by Tebex when enabled; we do not collect or store payment data ourselves.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected information for:

  • Moderation and rule enforcement (e.g., banning or investigating misconduct).
  • Maintaining server security and stability.
  • Communication with players regarding rule violations or appeals.
  • Limited community interaction (e.g., sending a message through Discord if needed).

We do not sell, share, or trade your personal data for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with third-party services used to operate BambooCraft:

  • Discord — Chat messages, usernames, and IDs (per Discord’s policies).
  • Tebex — For store transactions (you can view Tebex’s privacy policy on their website).

No other external analytics or tracking tools (such as bStats) are active.

5. Data Retention

Most logs (chat, commands, gameplay) are kept indefinitely for moderation and security reasons. Some data cannot be deleted due to how Minecraft server software stores logs.

IP addresses and identifiers are not linked together beyond what’s required for bans. If you wish to inquire about deletion of any data that can be removed, contact us at sbamboomcnetwork@gmail.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

While mild swearing is allowed on the server, BambooCraft is not specifically targeted at children under 13. Players under 13 should have parental permission to join.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any updates will be posted on web.bamboocraftmc.net. Continued use of our services means you accept the revised policy.

If you have questions or requests related to privacy, contact:
