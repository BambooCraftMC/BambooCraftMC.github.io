BambooCraftMC Server Rules

1. Be Respectful

You must respect all users, regardless of your liking towards them.

2. No Inappropriate Language

The use of profanity should be kept to a minimum.

3. No Spamming

Don't send a lot of small messages right after each other. Do not disrupt chat by spamming.

4. No NSFW Material

This is a community server and not meant to share pornographic, adult, or otherwise NSFW material.

5. No Advertisements

We do not tolerate any kind of advertisements, whether it be for other communities or streams.

6. Direct & Indirect Threats

Threats to other users such as DDoS, death, DoX, abuse, or other malicious acts are absolutely prohibited.

Enforcement

The Admins, Owners, and Mods may mute, kick, or ban at their discretion. If you feel mistreated, please DM an Admin and we will resolve the issue.

Clarifications

If you don't understand something, feel free to contact one of the owners!

Agreement

Your presence in this server implies acceptance of these rules, including all future changes. These changes may occur at any time without notice, and it is your responsibility to check for updates.
