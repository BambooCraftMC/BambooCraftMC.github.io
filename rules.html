BambooCraftMC Server Rules

1. Be Respectful

Be respectful to other players. Minor disrespect or heated arguments may occur, but excessive harassment is not tolerated.

  • Action: Mute on Discord and in-game (no warning issued).

2. No Inappropriate Language

The use of excessive profanity is not allowed, Don't swear too much

  • Action: Warning.

3. No Spamming

Do not send repeated or disruptive messages in chat.

  • Action: Mute.

4. No NSFW Material

This server is not meant for pornographic, adult, or otherwise NSFW content.

  • Action: 1-day in-game ban and mute on Discord.

5. No Advertisements

Advertising other servers, communities, or streams is not allowed.

  • Action: Warning.

6. Direct & Indirect Threats

Threats such as DDoS, death threats, doxxing, or other malicious acts are strictly prohibited.

  • Action: Warning.

Disciplinary System

The following system applies to warnings and bans:

  • Users may receive up to 3 warnings.
  • After 3 warnings, the user will be banned.
  • Bans are temporary and last a set number of days.
  • When banned, the user receives a permanent warning on record.
  • No permanent bans will be issued unless the user has reached 3 warnings.
  • ALL PAST BANS BEFORE THESE RULES STAND

Enforcement

Admins, Owners, and Mods enforce these rules. Punishments may vary slightly depending on severity or repetition.

Agreement

By participating in this server, you agree to these rules and any future updates.
